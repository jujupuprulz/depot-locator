Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Depot Locator application.

Information We Collect

When you use the Depot Locator application, we collect the following types of information:

  • Depot data that you create and manage within the application
  • Search queries for locations
  • Technical information such as browser type and device information

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Depot Locator application
  • Save your depot data for future use
  • Process and complete searches for depot coverage
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns and trends

Data Storage

Your depot data is stored in:

  • Your browser's local storage on your device
  • Our Supabase database if you choose to back up your data

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Netlify for hosting the application
  • Supabase for database storage
  • OpenStreetMap/Nominatim for geocoding and map services

Each of these services has their own privacy policies that govern how they handle your data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we have about you
  • Request that we delete any personal information we have about you
  • Object to the processing of your personal information
  • Data portability (receive your data in a structured, commonly used format)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.

Contact Us

For more information about our privacy practices, if you have questions, or if you would like to make a complaint, please contact us by email at jswtsao@gmail.com.
